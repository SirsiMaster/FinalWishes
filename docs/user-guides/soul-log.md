# Soul Log

> Your personal diary -- video, audio, or written reflections captured in the moment. Not a social media post. A private conversation with yourself and the people who matter to you.

## What You'll Need
- An active FinalWishes account with at least one estate
- A camera and/or microphone (for video and audio entries)

## Step by Step

### Creating an Entry

1. Navigate to your estate and click **Soul Log** in the sidebar
2. Click the **New Entry** button (the large blue button with a recording indicator)
3. Choose your entry type:
   - **Video** -- Record a video using your device's camera
   - **Audio** -- Record a voice memo using your microphone
   - **Written** -- Type a reflection using the rich text editor (bold, italic, headings, lists)
4. Optionally add:
   - **Title** -- A name for this entry (auto-generated if left blank, e.g. "May 19, 2026 -- Afternoon")
   - **Mood** -- Choose an emoji that captures how you're feeling
   - **Visibility** -- Who can see this entry (see below)
   - **Tagged People** -- Tag specific beneficiaries so they see the entry
5. Click **Save Entry**

### Recording Video or Audio

1. Your browser will ask for camera/microphone permission -- click **Allow**
2. Press the round **Record** button to start
3. A live timer shows how long you've been recording
4. Press the **Stop** button (square icon) when finished
5. Review your recording before saving -- click **Record again** if you want a redo
6. After saving, FinalWishes automatically transcribes your recording so it becomes searchable

### Visibility Settings

Each entry has one of three visibility levels:

| Visibility | Who Can See It | Icon |
|-----------|----------------|------|
| **Private** | Only you | Lock |
| **Shared** | You + tagged beneficiaries | People |
| **Sealed** | Delivered on a specific date or upon your passing | Envelope |

**Sealed entries** are special -- they are kept hidden until a trigger you choose:
- **On a specific date** -- Choose a future date (great for birthdays or anniversaries)
- **On my passing** -- Delivered to tagged people after the estate enters settlement

### Browsing Your Entries

- Entries appear in reverse chronological order (newest first)
- Use the **search bar** to find entries by title, content, or transcript text
- Click any entry card to expand and see the full content
- Video entries show a thumbnail -- click to open the full video player
- Audio entries show a waveform player with play/pause controls

### The Shepherd

The Shepherd is your AI companion in the Soul Log. Each day it offers a different reflection prompt to inspire your entries:
- "What's a lesson you learned the hard way that you'd want to pass on?"
- "What does home mean to you?"
- "If you could relive one day, which would it be?"

If you haven't recorded in over 7 days, the Shepherd will gently remind you.

## Tips
- You don't need to say anything profound. Even 30 seconds of "how your day went" becomes precious to your family.
- Written reflections are always free, regardless of your plan tier. Video and audio may be limited on the free plan.
- Name your entries clearly -- they become easier to find later.
- Use **Sealed** entries for birthday messages, graduation congratulations, or anything you want delivered at the right moment.

## FAQ

**Q: Can my family see my private entries?**
A: No. Private entries are visible only to you. Shared entries are visible to you and anyone you tag. Sealed entries are hidden until their delivery trigger.

**Q: What happens to my entries if I pass away?**
A: Private entries remain private (your executor cannot see them unless you change the visibility). Shared entries remain visible to tagged people. Sealed entries are delivered according to your chosen trigger.

**Q: Are my video and audio recordings transcribed?**
A: Yes. After saving, FinalWishes sends the recording to our transcription service. Transcripts typically appear within a few minutes and make your entries searchable by spoken words.

**Q: What if my recording fails to transcribe?**
A: You'll see a "Transcription failed" message with a **Retry** button. Click it to try again. Your original recording is never affected.
