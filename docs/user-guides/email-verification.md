# Email Verification

## What It Does
After you create your FinalWishes account, we send a verification link to your email address. This confirms that you own the email and helps protect your account.

## What You'll See
If your email isn't yet verified, you'll see a **gold banner** at the top of your dashboard that says:

> **Please verify your email address**
> Check [your email] for a verification link.

## How to Verify

### Step 1: Check Your Email
Look for an email from FinalWishes with the subject "Verify your email address."

### Step 2: Click the Link
Click the verification link in the email. It will open a confirmation page.

### Step 3: Done!
The gold banner will disappear and your email is now verified.

## Didn't Get the Email?
- Click the **"Resend Email"** button on the gold banner
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Make sure the email address you registered with is correct

## Why Does This Matter?
- Verified emails help us send you important estate notifications
- Password reset requires a verified email
- Some features may require email verification before use

## Can I Dismiss the Banner?
Yes — click the **×** button on the right side of the banner. It will reappear on your next visit until you verify.
