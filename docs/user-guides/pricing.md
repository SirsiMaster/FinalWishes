# Plans & Pricing

> FinalWishes offers three tiers so you can choose the level of support that fits your needs -- from a free starter plan to full-service estate organization.

## What You'll Need
- A FinalWishes account (you can explore the free tier immediately after signing up)

## The Plans

### Guardian (Free)
Everything you need to get started:
- Create one estate
- Add up to 10 assets
- Upload up to 5 documents to the vault
- Add beneficiaries (executors and heirs)
- Write one final directive
- Basic encryption for all stored data

### Concierge ($29/month)
For families ready to organize their full legacy:
- Everything in Guardian, plus:
- Unlimited assets and documents
- Digital Lockbox for secure credential storage
- Time Capsules with scheduled delivery
- Video Memorials and photo galleries
- AI-assisted obituary drafting (The Shepherd)
- Priority email support

### White Glove ($99/month)
Premium service for comprehensive estate planning:
- Everything in Concierge, plus:
- Heirloom Registry with provenance tracking
- E-signature integration for legal documents
- Multi-estate management
- Dedicated support line
- SOC 2 certified security compliance
- Advanced reporting and estate analytics

## How to Upgrade
1. Click your profile icon in the top right corner
2. Select **Settings**, then navigate to **Subscription**
3. Click **Upgrade** next to the plan you'd like
4. You'll be taken to a secure payment page powered by Stripe
5. Enter your payment details and confirm
6. Your new features are available immediately

## Tips
- Start with Guardian to explore the platform, then upgrade when you're ready for more.
- Concierge is the most popular plan for families actively organizing their estate.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. You can downgrade or cancel from your Settings page at any time. Your data remains safe even if you return to the free tier.
