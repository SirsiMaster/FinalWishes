# Your Security

> Your estate information is among the most sensitive data you'll ever store online. Here's how FinalWishes protects it.

## What You'll Need
- No action required -- these protections are built into every FinalWishes account

## How Your Data Is Protected

### Encryption at Rest (AES-256)
Every document, credential, and piece of sensitive information you store is encrypted using AES-256 -- the same standard used by banks and government agencies. In plain terms: your data is scrambled into an unreadable format, and only an authorized key can unscramble it. Even if someone accessed our storage directly, they would see only encrypted noise.

### Cloud Key Management (Cloud KMS)
The keys that encrypt your data are themselves protected by Google Cloud's Key Management Service. This means the encryption keys are stored in a separate, hardened system -- not alongside your data. It's a lockbox inside a lockbox.

### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA ensures that even if someone learns your password, they still can't access your account without the 6-digit code from your authenticator app. MFA is required for executors, heirs, and legal professionals.

### Role-Based Access
FinalWishes uses strict role-based access controls:
- **You (the Principal)** have full access to your estate
- **Executors** can only access your estate after your passing is confirmed and a 72-hour cooling-off period ends
- **Heirs** can only see items specifically designated to them
- No one at FinalWishes can view your data

### SOC 2 Compliance
SOC 2 is an industry audit standard that verifies a company handles your data securely. It covers five areas: security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. FinalWishes is built to meet this standard, so you can trust that your information is handled with the highest level of care.

## Tips
- Enable MFA on your account even if it's not required for your role. It's the single most effective step you can take.
- Use a strong, unique password for your FinalWishes account. Don't reuse passwords from other services.

## FAQ
**Q: Can FinalWishes employees see my documents or passwords?**
A: No. Your data is encrypted with keys managed by Google Cloud KMS. FinalWishes staff cannot decrypt or view your information.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel my account?**
A: Your data remains encrypted and stored for a grace period. You can request permanent deletion at any time through your Settings page under Privacy.
