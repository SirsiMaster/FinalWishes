# Inviting Team Members

## What It Does
As an estate owner, you can invite other people to help manage your estate. Each person is assigned a specific role that determines what they can see and do.

## Available Roles

| Role | What They Can Do | MFA Required? |
|------|-----------------|:-------------:|
| **Beneficiary / Heir** | View estate information (read-only) | ✅ Yes |
| **Executor** | View and manage assets, documents, and letters | ✅ Yes |
| **Legal Counsel** | View estate information (read-only advisory) | ✅ Yes |
| **CPA / Tax Advisor** | View estate information (read-only advisory) | ✅ Yes |

> **Note:** All invited team members must complete two-factor authentication (MFA) and sign an identity attestation before they can access estate data.

## How to Invite Someone

### Step 1: Go to Estate Settings
Navigate to your estate dashboard and click **Settings** in the sidebar.

### Step 2: Find the "Estate Team" Section
Scroll down to the Estate Team section. You'll see any existing members and a button to invite new ones.

### Step 3: Click "Invite Team Member"
Click the dashed "Invite Team Member" button.

### Step 4: Fill in Their Details
- **Full Name**: Their legal name
- **Email Address**: The email they'll use to create their FinalWishes account
- **Role**: Select the appropriate role from the four options

### Step 5: Click "Send Invitation"
The invitation will be saved and they'll know to create an account.

## What Happens Next
1. The invitee creates a FinalWishes account using the same email
2. They'll be prompted to set up two-factor authentication (MFA)
3. They'll need to complete an identity attestation (digital signature)
4. Once verified, they'll have access to your estate based on their role

## Managing Invitations

### Viewing Status
Each team member shows a status badge:
- 🟡 **Pending** — Invitation sent, waiting for them to register
- 🟢 **Active** — They've accepted and completed verification
- 🔴 **Declined** — They chose not to accept
- ⚪ **Revoked** — You cancelled their invitation

### Revoking an Invitation
Hover over a pending invitation and click **Revoke** to cancel it. This removes their access before they can accept.

## Who Can Invite?
Only **estate owners** (principals) can invite team members. Executors, heirs, and advisors cannot invite others.
