# Time Capsules

> Schedule messages, photos, and videos to be delivered to your loved ones at exactly the right moment -- whether that's a specific date, a milestone, or after your passing.

## What You'll Need
- An active FinalWishes account with at least one estate
- At least one heir or family member added to your estate

## Step by Step

### Creating a Time Capsule
1. Navigate to your estate dashboard and click **Time Capsules** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Capsule**
3. Write your message using the rich text editor
4. Optionally attach photos, videos, or documents from your vault
5. Choose a recipient from your list of heirs
6. Select a delivery trigger (see below)
7. Click **Save as Draft** or **Seal Capsule**

### Delivery Types
- **Specific Date** -- Delivered on the exact date you choose (e.g., a child's 18th birthday)
- **Upon Passing** -- Released after your passing is confirmed by your executor
- **Upon Settlement** -- Delivered when your estate reaches the settlement phase
- **Anniversary** -- Sent on a recurring date (e.g., every wedding anniversary)

### Sealing a Capsule
When you seal a capsule, it becomes locked and scheduled for delivery. After sealing:
- You have a **72-hour window** to unseal and make changes
- After 72 hours, the capsule is permanently sealed

### Cancelling a Capsule
You can cancel any capsule that hasn't been delivered yet:
1. Open the capsule from your list
2. If it's within the 72-hour window, click **Unseal**
3. To cancel entirely, click **Delete Capsule**

### What Recipients Receive
When a time capsule is delivered, the recipient receives:
- An email notification letting them know a message is waiting
- Access to the capsule content within their FinalWishes account
- Any attached photos, videos, or documents

## Tips
- Record a video message and attach it to a capsule for a deeply personal touch.
- Consider creating capsules for major life milestones: graduations, weddings, first child.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I see what the recipient will receive?**
A: Yes. Click **Preview** on any capsule to see exactly what the recipient will see.

**Q: What if I change my mind after sealing?**
A: You have 72 hours to unseal and edit or delete the capsule. After that, contact support for assistance.
