# The Final Record

> Write your own obituary -- or let our AI assistant help you draft one -- so your life story is told the way you want it told.

## What You'll Need
- An active FinalWishes account with at least one estate

## Step by Step

### Writing Your Obituary
1. Navigate to your estate dashboard and click **Obituary** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a rich text editor where you can write freely
3. Include the details that matter to you: your life story, accomplishments, family, passions, and anything you want remembered
4. Click **Save Draft** at any time -- your work is automatically preserved

### Using AI Assist
Not sure where to start? The Shepherd, our AI assistant, can help:
1. Click the **AI Assist** button
2. The Shepherd will generate a draft based on the information in your estate -- your name, family members, and any details you've already added
3. Review the draft and edit it to match your voice and wishes
4. The AI provides a starting point; the final words are always yours

### Review and Sign
When your obituary is complete:
1. Click **Finalize**
2. Review the full text one last time
3. You'll be guided through a digital signing process to confirm this is your approved version
4. Once signed, the obituary is locked and stored securely

### Sharing with Family
- You can choose to share your finalized obituary with your executor so they have it ready when needed
- Your executor can also share it with funeral homes, newspapers, or memorial services at the appropriate time

## Tips
- Write in your own voice. This is your story -- it doesn't have to follow a formula.
- Ask a trusted family member to review your draft for any details you may have missed.
- You can update your obituary as life changes. Just unseal it, edit, and re-finalize.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I keep my obituary private?**
A: Yes. By default, only you can see it. You choose whether to share it with your executor.

**Q: Will the AI write the whole thing for me?**
A: The AI generates a starting draft. You should review and personalize it -- nobody tells your story better than you.
