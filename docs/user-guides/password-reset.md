# Password Reset

## What It Does
If you've forgotten your password, you can request a password reset link sent to your email. This lets you create a new password without needing to contact support.

## How to Reset Your Password

### Step 1: Go to the Login Page
Navigate to the FinalWishes sign-in page.

### Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?"
Below the password field, you'll see a "Forgot password?" link. Click it.

### Step 3: Enter Your Email
Type the email address you used when you created your account.

### Step 4: Click "Send Reset Link"
You'll see a gold button — click it to send the reset email.

### Step 5: Check Your Email
Look for an email with the subject "Reset your password." Click the link inside.

### Step 6: Create a New Password
Follow the instructions to set a new password. We recommend at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

### Step 7: Sign In
Go back to the login page and sign in with your new password.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Didn't receive the email | Check your spam/junk folder, then try again |
| Link expired | Request a new reset link — they expire after 1 hour |
| Still can't get in | Contact your estate administrator for help |

## Security Note
For your protection, we show the same confirmation message regardless of whether an account exists for that email. This prevents anyone from discovering which email addresses have FinalWishes accounts.
