# Data Privacy & Security

## How FinalWishes Protects Your Information

FinalWishes uses multiple layers of security to protect your estate information. Here's what you need to know.

## Who Can See What

### Estate Owners (Principals)
You have **full control** over your estate. You can:
- View and edit all estate information
- Add, modify, or remove assets and documents
- Manage executors and beneficiaries
- Read identity verifications from your team members
- View audit logs of all activity on your estate

### Executors
Executors can:
- View estate information, assets, and documents
- Add and update assets and documents
- Create letters and notifications

Executors **cannot**:
- Delete documents (to preserve legal evidence)
- Change estate ownership
- Remove other team members

### Heirs / Beneficiaries
Heirs can:
- View the estate dashboard and their designated assets
- View documents shared with them

Heirs **cannot**:
- Edit, add, or delete any estate information
- Access documents not shared with them

### Legal Counsel & CPAs
Legal and CPA advisors can:
- View estate information relevant to their advisory role

They **cannot**:
- Modify any estate data
- Access information outside their assigned estate

## How Your Data Is Protected

### Encryption
- **At rest**: All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **In transit**: All connections use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
- **Sensitive data**: Social Security numbers, account numbers, and other PII are stored in a separate secure database with additional encryption

### Authentication
- **Email + Password**: Required for all users
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Required for executors, heirs, legal, and CPA users
- **Identity Attestation**: Fiduciary users must sign a legal declaration before accessing estate data

### Audit Trail
- Every action on your estate is logged permanently
- You can review who accessed what and when
- Audit logs cannot be edited or deleted — they are permanent records

## What We Never Do
- We **never** sell your data
- We **never** share your data with third parties without your consent
- We **never** store passwords in plain text
- We **never** expose sensitive information like SSNs or account numbers in URLs or logs

## Your Rights
- You can download your data at any time
- You can request account deletion (coming soon -- contact support@finalwishes.app in the meantime)
- You can revoke access for any team member at any time
- You control who is part of your estate team

## Questions?
If you have questions about how your data is protected, contact us at **support@finalwishes.app**.
