# FinalWishes on iPhone (in development)

FinalWishes is becoming a native **iPhone app**, so you can organize your
estate, record your voice, and keep your vault with you — the same FinalWishes
you use on the web, in an app you install from the App Store.

## What to expect

- **Everything from the web, on your phone.** The app is the full FinalWishes
  experience — your vault, memoirs, beneficiaries, and the Shepherd's guidance.
- **Sign in the same way** you do on the web, with the same account.
- **Your data is the same.** The app and the website share one secure account;
  changes on one show up on the other.

## Plans & payment

For now, the iPhone app does **not** show plans or take payment. If you want to
upgrade to a paid plan, do it on the website
([finalwishes-prod.web.app](https://finalwishes-prod.web.app)); your upgrade
applies to the app automatically. Paying inside the iPhone app will come later.

## Availability

The iPhone app is in active development and is not yet on the App Store. This
guide will be updated with install steps when it's available.
