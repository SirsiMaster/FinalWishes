# Managing Your Assets

> Keep a complete inventory of everything you own -- from bank accounts to family heirlooms -- so your loved ones know exactly what exists and where to find it.

## What You'll Need
- An active FinalWishes account with at least one estate

## Step by Step

### Adding an Asset
1. Navigate to your estate dashboard and click **Assets** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Add Asset** button
3. Choose a category:
   - **Financial** -- bank accounts, investments, insurance policies
   - **Real Estate** -- homes, land, rental properties
   - **Vehicles** -- cars, boats, motorcycles
   - **Digital** -- websites, domain names, online accounts with monetary value
   - **Personal Property** -- jewelry, artwork, furniture, collectibles
4. Fill in the details. Each category has fields specific to that type of asset (for example, Real Estate asks for the property address and type).
5. Enter an **estimated value** to help calculate your total estate worth
6. Optionally, designate an heir -- the person you'd like to receive this asset
7. Click **Save**

### Editing Asset Details
1. Click on any asset in your list to open its detail view
2. Update any field and click **Save Changes**
3. You can also attach related documents from your vault (e.g., a deed for a property)

### Viewing Your Asset Inventory
Your asset list shows all items organized by category. At the top of the page, you'll see your **total estimated estate value** -- the sum of all your assets.

## Tips
- Link supporting documents to each asset (deeds, titles, policy numbers) so everything is in one place.
- Review your asset list annually to keep values and details current.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I assign the same asset to more than one heir?**
A: Each asset is designated to one heir. For shared assets, note the arrangement in the asset's description field.

**Q: What happens to assets when my estate enters settlement?**
A: Your Executor will see the full asset inventory and designated heirs to guide the distribution process.
