# Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

## What It Does
Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your FinalWishes account. After entering your email and password, you'll also need to enter a 6-digit code from an authenticator app on your phone.

## Who Needs It
- **Estate Owners (Principals)**: Optional but recommended
- **Heirs, Executors, Legal Professionals, CPAs**: **Required** before you can access estate documents

## How to Enable 2FA

### Step 1: Open Settings
Navigate to your **Account Settings** page from the sidebar menu.

### Step 2: Find the Two-Factor Authentication Section
At the top of Settings, you'll see a section labeled **Two-Factor Authentication** with a gold left border.

### Step 3: Click "Enable 2FA"
This will generate a QR code on screen.

### Step 4: Scan the QR Code
Open your authenticator app and scan the QR code:
- **Google Authenticator** (recommended)
- **1Password**
- **Authy**
- **Microsoft Authenticator**

### Step 5: Enter the 6-Digit Code
Your authenticator app will show a 6-digit code that changes every 30 seconds. Enter this code in the verification field and click **Verify & Activate**.

### Step 6: Done!
You'll see a green "Active" badge. From now on, you'll be asked for this code each time you log in.

## What Happens When You Log In with 2FA
1. Enter your email/username and password as usual
2. You'll see a new screen asking for your 6-digit authenticator code
3. Open your authenticator app, find the FinalWishes entry, and enter the current code
4. You're in!

## Can I Remove 2FA?
- **Estate Owners**: Yes, go to Settings and click "Remove 2FA"
- **Heirs/Executors/Legal/CPAs**: No. 2FA is required for your role to protect the estate you have access to.

## Troubleshooting
| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Lost your phone | Contact your estate principal to request access recovery |
| Code not working | Make sure your phone's clock is accurate (codes are time-based) |
| Can't scan QR code | Try adjusting your phone's camera position or screen brightness |
